  • Security

A Tip jar is a special address that's not protected by your password. This lets you tip easier but increases your security risk. If you use the Tip Jar you should only keep an amount you're willing to lose in it.

We'll generate a warning when your Tip Jar balance gets too high. Here you can set the maximum percent of your total wallet balance you Tip Jar should contain. For instance, if you choose 10% and you have a total balance of 20,000, we'll warn you when your tip jar contains more than 2,000.

This works like 'Tip Jar Max Balance %'. Use this setting to ensure that even as your wallet grows, you don't end up with too high of a balance in your unprotected Tip Jar.

If you're making a small payment from your Tip Jar, we can send it without showing a confirmation prompt. The prompt can be annoying when you're in a hurry, but helpful to prevent mistakes. Set a limit here to control when you get a prompt.
